**Why Hover Translator?**
Reading in a foreign language shouldn't mean constant interruptions. Hover Translator lets you understand any word on any webpage in under a second — no copy-pasting, no new tabs, no losing your place. Just hover.

**What it does**

**Instant hover translations** Move your cursor over any word and a translation appears instantly. The tooltip disappears when you move on. Zero friction, zero workflow disruption.

**Context-aware results** Hover Translator doesn't just look up the word in isolation — it reads the surrounding sentence to give you the translation that fits the context. "Interest" in a banking article translates differently than in a dating profile. It gets that right.

**Multiple meanings, all at once** The tooltip shows the primary translation plus a set of alternatives, so you can pick the right nuance without opening a dictionary. Part-of-speech labels (noun, verb, adj.) tell you exactly how the word is being used.

**Works in 30+ languages** English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Dutch, Polish, Russian, Chinese, Japanese, Korean, Arabic, Turkish, Swedish, and more. Switch your target language in one click from the popup.

**Smart language detection** No need to tell the extension what language a page is in. It detects it automatically and routes the translation accordingly — including pivot translations for language pairs that translate better via an intermediate language.

**German separable verbs** One of the hardest things about reading German. Hover Translator recognises split verbs like stattfinden even when the prefix appears at the end of the clause ("findet… statt") and gives you the full infinitive and its meaning.

**Proper noun awareness** Hover over a name or a place and the extension knows not to translate it. When a word is a well-known entity, it surfaces a short Wikipedia-style description instead of a wrong translation.

**Works everywhere** News sites, Wikipedia, social media, academic papers, Reddit threads, product pages — if text is on the page, it translates it.

**Excluded sites list** Don't want it active on your email client or coding editor? Add those domains to the blacklist and the extension stays silent there.

**Adjustable tooltip delay** Set exactly how long you need to hover before the tooltip appears — from 100 ms to 1 second.
